About

I was supposed to be spending this semester (Spring 2020) studying abroad in China, but for reasons you are probably well aware of by now, my program was cancelled. Rather than going back to UW-Madison for the semester, I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp through UCSD-Extension. I will be returning to UW-Madison in the fall. While it is still a major work in progress, this site will serve as an exhibition for my projects and accomplishments as I embark on a journey to learn the ins and outs of software development.
